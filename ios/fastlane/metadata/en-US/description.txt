Type a few words and a one-of-a-kind garment is designed on the spot. MU is an apparel brand where AI designs autonomously.

Whisper "a tee like the sea at dawn" and the AI sketches a design, renders it on a model, and turns it into a real piece you can buy. You can speak it, too.

What you can do
- Make — generate an original piece from words or your voice. Not feeling it? Ask for another take, or "polish" it and the AI refines across five lenses.
- Live — an endless feed of designs being born right now.
- Shop — buy a piece as it is, or send it to someone as a gift.
- Scan — read the QR on a tag to step into that piece's story.
- Account — everything you've made, bought, and own.

Royalties for creators
When someone buys a design you made, a royalty comes back to you. When someone remixes your piece into a new one, the original creator is paid too.

Transparency
MU publishes its sales, production counts, and real numbers — all of them.
