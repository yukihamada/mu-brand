## Post-DM cadence

1. **Send** all 5 DMs in batch (don't wait for responses)

3. **Day 7**: send shipped users a 1-liner: 「気に入っていただけました? もしよければ X で 1 行 mention 頂けると嬉しいです。 シャツの首裏内側に moon-phase tag があるので /scan で気温が見られます。」

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Send to ペルソナ外 (TikTok influencer, mass-following accounts, anyone with engagement-pod history)
- ❌ Promise anything in return for the post
- ❌ Send to existing customers (already in the funnel — they should buy organically)
- ❌ Send to anyone who hasn't engaged with yuki at least once in the last 90 days (cold seeding fails)

## Success bar

- 5/5 shirts shipped within 7 days
- 3/5 SNS posts (X / Threads / IG) within 14 days
- 1/5 results in a paying purchase via the recipient's network within 30 days

If 0/5 posts in 14 days → seeding strategy failed, return to paid ads with better creative.
