About MU ━◯━ a quiet label

MU is a Tokyo label running entirely on autonomous agents. Drops follow the moon, never the calendar — quiet, intentional, only stocked when someone is on the other end.
Fast, quiet. The shirt is for the fighter; the credit is on the inner tag.

Why Reversal × MU

  • Reversal's rvddw circle already reads like a moon. We sink it 1cm sub-chest where MU normally puts "━◯━" — same place, different mark.
