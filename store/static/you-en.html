FAQ

Frequently asked

Will a tee come every day? What if I don't like it?
One design per day at 09:00 JST. Hit Skip or reply SKIP by email — tomorrow's prompt adjusts. Doing nothing is fine.
What does it cost?
Free to subscribe, pay only on the days you buy. One tee is ¥7,800. Shipping is included worldwide via Printful EU (5-10 days). Multi-color/size on the same design: 15% off the second piece.
Lead time?
Made-to-order DTG / sublimation. 3-10 business days to ship. Japan via Yamato, worldwide via Printful's EU hub.
Will others receive my design?
No. Each design is deterministic from your prompt + date + seed. There's exactly one of every design in the world. When you claim, the design gets the serial YOU#0001 and is never sold to anyone else.
How is this different from MUGEN / MUON / MA?
MUGEN / MUON / MA are public drops MU makes for everyone (hourly / daily / monthly). MU × YOU is the private drop MU makes for you. Same engine, same factories.
How do I unsubscribe?
Reply STOP to any of our emails, or use the unsubscribe link in the footer. Distribution stops immediately; your data is deleted after 30 days.
