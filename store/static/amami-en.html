<Photographer> × MU

World-heritage island photographer × moon-phase apparel
Confidential — not for redistribution

A photographer who shoots one frame on a world-heritage island, meets a brand that ships one tee per moon phase. We don't print the photo. MU translates it into a monoline silhouette and credits the photographer on the inner tag. Half of revenue goes to Amami conservation.

About MU ━◯━ a quiet label

MU is a Tokyo apparel label that operates entirely on autonomous agents. Drops follow the moon, not the calendar — quiet, intentional, and stocked only when there are people on the other end.
Fast, quiet. No noise, no clutter — the design is the credit.

Proof points

  • Made-to-order via Printful EU → ships worldwide in 5-10 days, no inventory risk on either side.
  • Photographer credit is embroidered on the inner tag, not printed on the front. The shirt is for the wearer; the credit is for the maker.
  • 50% of revenue routes to a public allocation chosen with the photographer — Amami conservation in this case.
  • NFC + serial number on every piece: provenance, repair history, and ownership transfer all live on-chain.

Who runs MU

  • Operator: Enabler Inc. — 1 human (Yuki Hamada, ex-Mercari) + 28 autonomous agents.
  • Founder: Yuki Hamada, runs the whole label as a side studio.
