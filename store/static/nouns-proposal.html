Nouns DAO Proposal

MU × NOUNS

The First Autonomous AI Fashion Collab.
Three tracks. Zero funding requested.
Just a cosign from the DAO.
TL;DR — MU is an autonomous AI fashion brand: designs generated every hour, every day, every month, no human creative direction. We want to weave ⌐◨-◨ into that machine, donate 10% of collab drops to the Nouns Treasury, and run a monthly auction using the exact Nouns mechanism. Zero funding requested. Just a handshake.

Motivation

Nouns proved something important: if you make the process the product, the community builds the culture around it. MU is trying to do the same thing for fashion — an autonomous AI pipeline (Gemini → Printful → Solana NFT) that generates garments on a fixed cadence, no trend chasing, no creative director, no hype cycle.

The glasses are CC0. The mechanism is open. MU's pipeline is already running. This collab is a natural fit: two projects that trust process over people, permanence over virality.

We're not here to slap a logo on a hoodie. We're here to let the machine do something strange and interesting with one of the most recognizable pixel assets in crypto — and share the upside with the DAO.

Three Tracks

Track 1
MUGEN × NOUNS — Weekly
Every week, MU's AI generates a new streetwear graphic that reinterprets ⌐◨-◨ as a source element — not a direct print, but a structural input to the generation prompt. The output is weird, generative, and on-chain certified. CC0 means we can do this without asking permission, but we'd rather have the DAO's blessing than operate in a gray zone.
Track 2
MUON × NOUNS — Daily, Climate-Gated
MUON drops happen daily. The quantity printed per drop equals the current temperature in Hokkaido, Japan — a natural oracle creating real scarcity tied to weather. MUON × NOUNS editions run inside this system: 10% of gross sales from each collab drop go directly to the Nouns Treasury. Climate determines supply. The DAO gets a cut. No human decides either.
Track 3
MA × NOUNS Auction — Monthly
MA is MU's monthly release — one piece, highest bid wins, 24-hour window, no reserve. The mechanism is lifted directly from Nouns. MA is fashion's Nouns. One piece per month, on-chain auction, 10% of the winning bid to the Nouns Treasury. We want the DAO to formally recognize this as a recurring contribution, not a one-time gesture.
