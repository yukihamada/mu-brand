Why this proliferates the meme

  1. A public good, not a private store. Today, any builder who wants to put a Noun on a tee has to rebuild upscaling, print-file generation, mockup preview, and fulfillment plumbing from scratch. We will open-source the entire Noun-ID → print pipeline under CC0 — the same license as Nouns art itself. After this prop, any Nouner can self-host "wear your Noun" with no dependency on MU. That is proliferation infrastructure the DAO owns forever.
  2. Japan is a Nouns gap. Nouns' Japan footprint is a handful of event touchpoints (Prop 559 — EDCON 2024 Tokyo; DAO Tokyo). There is no standing way for someone in Tokyo to walk away wearing a Noun. MU is a Tokyo-based, fully operational apparel engine with global Printful fulfillment already live. We are the cheapest possible wedge into Asia.
  3. Bodies > banners. The deliverable is physical Nouns in the wild — free tees on Nouners and on Tokyo crowds, each a CC0 Noun with a QR back to nouns.wtf. Cheap, measurable, exactly the "spread the meme" mandate.
