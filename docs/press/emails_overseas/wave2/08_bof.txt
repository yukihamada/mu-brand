Pitching for BoF Professional / BoF Sustainability beat.

**Story shape:** MU (https://wearmu.com) is a Tokyo-based apparel
brand launched 2026-05-07. Two things make it BoF-shaped:

**1. Demand forecasting is structurally removed from the supply curve.**
Production volume each day equals the temperature, in degrees Celsius,
of a small Hokkaido town (Teshikaga). 25°C = 25 pieces, −10°C = 10
pieces. A weather oracle determines run size. The intent is to break
the prediction-bias-driven overproduction problem that BoF has covered
extensively — there is no buyer in the loop and no trend forecast to
miss. Made-to-order via Printful keeps the inventory cost at zero.

**2. Bonding-curve pricing on physical goods.**
First buyer pays ¥5,000 (~$33). Each subsequent buyer pays ¥250 more
(~$1.70). Capped at ¥30,000 (~$200). Inverted Dutch auction — early
discovery is economically rewarded. Web3 pricing mechanics on Stripe,
no token issued.

Three lines:
- MUGEN 無限 — hourly drops, 108-piece cycle
- MUON 無音 — daily, weather-gated supply
- 間 MA   — monthly, single-piece auction (mechanism lifted from
            Nouns DAO)

Founder: Yuki Hamada — ex-CPO of Mercari (2014–2021, IPO 2018), ex-

The entire engine is MIT-licensed open-source (github.com/yukihamada/
mu-brand), so the operating model is forkable. We've already shipped
a derivative ("MU CITY") that swaps the weather oracle for Tokyo
foot-traffic data.

Happy to share week-1 sales distribution data under embargo if useful
