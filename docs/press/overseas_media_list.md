## Sending principles

1. **Subject ≤ 70 chars**, hook in first 6 words.
2. **English** for Tier 1–4, Japanese only for Japan-resident outlets.
3. **Currency: show JPY → USD** conversion next to every price (₩...→$...).
4. **First paragraph is the whole story**. Editors don't read past line 3.
5. **Always offer** the founder for interview, demo video, or live-pipeline shoulder-look.
6. **No BCC**. Each outlet gets a personalised opener referencing their beat.
7. **Embargo**: none — brand is already public since 2026-05-07.
